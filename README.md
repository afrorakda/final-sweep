# #58 Final Sweep

A minimalist, high-performance "Final Sweep" tool for writers. Instantly highlights NG words and flags long sentences to polish your draft into a masterpiece.

## Core Philosophy
* **The Final Sweeper**: Designed as the "Last Rite" before submission. It cleans up your writing habits and structural clutter.
* **1 Tool / 2 Actions**: Combines NG word detection and sentence length checking in a single, fluid interface.
* **Privacy First**: Your draft stays in your browser. No server uploads, no data tracking.

## Key Features
* **Multi-Format NG Filter**: Detects "crutch words" or prohibited terms separated by spaces, commas, or Japanese punctuation.
* **Live Length Slider**: Adjust the maximum sentence length (40 to 150 chars) in real-time to visualize readability.
* **Zero-Ghost Overlay**: A perfectly synchronized dual-layer editor that aligns highlights with your text without visual lag or displacement.
* **Universal Support**: Optimized for both English and Japanese punctuation/sentence structures.

## How to Use
1. **Set NG Words**: Paste your forbidden words or personal "echo" habits in the top box.
2. **Set Threshold**: Use the slider to define what you consider "too long" for a single sentence.
3. **Paste & Sweep**: Drop your draft into the main editor. NG words appear in **yellow**, and long sentences get a **red underline**.
4. **Final Check**: Refine your text until the "NG FOUND" and "LONG SENT" counts hit zero.

## Design System
* **Background**: #fff
* **Main Color**: #333
* **Input Area**: #fafafa
* **Border Radius**: 15px (Fridge Combo Standard)
* **Performance**: Optimized for long-form text processing on both Mobile and Desktop.

## Privacy Policy
* No login required.
* No tracking scripts.
* No advertisements.
* Browser-only execution.
